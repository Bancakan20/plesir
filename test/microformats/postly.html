Terminal 3 is the best terminal in Soekarno-Hatta Airport. It's different than other terminals in the airport. It's small, yet it's really clean. It's the bigest improvements on air transportation services in Indonesia
Reviewed by: Didiet Noor <lynxluna@gmail.com> at January 04, 2011

This terminal is the big improvement of PT Angkasa Pura on improving services to the passenger. Terminal 3 is for Mandala and Air Asia The terminal served domestic flight only. The terminal is relatively quiet compared to its older brothers, terminal 1 and terminal 2.

What I really like about this terminal is the existence of "Meeting Point". Meeting point is the place for passenger and its company to meet. Passengers can check in, get a seat on the aeroplane and before boarding, they can hang out on this place
